Manananggal
The Tale

Beginning

In a quiet coastal town called Dalisay, the nights were often filled with crashing waves and howling winds. The villagers lived simple lives, but they carried a deep fear of the Manananggal, a creature said to separate its upper body from its legs and fly under the cover of darkness. Parents warned their children to stay indoors after sunset, and doors were always tightly locked before the moon rose high in the sky.
