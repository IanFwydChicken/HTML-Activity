Teens and social media use: What's the impact?

The Mayo Clinic article examines both the advantages and disadvantages of social media for teens‚Äô mental health. Social media can foster communication, creativity, and community, allowing teens to connect with peers who share similar interests or challenges. It also serves as a platform for educational content and self-expression.

However, the article also emphasizes the risks. Excessive use may lead to disrupted sleep, cyberbullying, social anxiety, and depression. The constant comparison to curated online personas can negatively affect self-esteem and body image.

Mayo Clinic recommends strategies to mitigate these risks, such as setting time limits, encouraging face-to-face interaction, monitoring content, and promoting healthy online habits. The article stresses that guidance from parents and caregivers is essential for fostering positive outcomes while reducing harm.
