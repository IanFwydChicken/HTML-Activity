Bacon And Eggs

    Needed Ingredients
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. Gather fresh ingredients: eggs, bacon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Preheat a skillet over medium heat for even cooking.
  3. Cook the bacon until crispy, then remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. In the same skillet, crack the eggs and cook to your desired doneness.
  5. Season the eggs with salt and pepper for added flavor.
  6. Serve the bacon and eggs together on a plate, optionally with toast or vegetables.
