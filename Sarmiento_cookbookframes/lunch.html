Sinigang

    Needed Ingredients
  • Pork belly or ribs
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Sinigang mix
  • water
  • Vegetable (radish, eggplant, kangkong, etc.,)
  1. Gather ingredients: pork (belly or ribs), water, Knorr Sinigang mix, tomatoes, onions, and vegetables (like radish, eggplant, and kangkong).
  2. Boil water in a pot and add the pork; simmer until tender.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and onions; cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the Knorr Sinigang mix for flavor.
  5. Incorporate the vegetables and cook until they are tender.
  6. Adjust seasoning with fish sauce or salt to taste, then serve hot.
